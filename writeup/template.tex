\begin{abstract}
We present a Ks-band imaging reduction pipeline for the black hole
low-mass X-ray binary GX~339-4, using HAWK-I at the ESO VLT.
ZOGY proper image subtraction is applied to isolate the variability
of the donor star from contaminating field sources.
\end{abstract}
